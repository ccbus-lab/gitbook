# 欧易OKX Web3钱包发币教程

使用OKX Web3钱包发行代币

考虑到很多用户不方便使用电脑，这篇教程主要是教大家如何使用OKX Web3钱包发行代币

### **1、找到PandaTool**

我们需要打开自己的OKX Web3钱包，然后在浏览器的【发现】位置搜索PandaTool，就能看到我们的DAPP了

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FLJUmKekXdLsoTIjX9U15%252F%25E5%258F%2591%25E7%258E%25B0%25E5%25AE%2598%25E7%25BD%2591.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Def5ed04b-a0cf-4f49-a988-b022e6bfafd5&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=6c46eba1&sv=2)

如果搜索不到，也可以直接输入PandaTool的官网域名进入：pandatool.org

### **2、切换链**

进入pandatool官网之后，我们可以自由切换链。你想在哪条链发币，就选择哪条链

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FIDKmqgAIBXfO93BD4hxy%252Fphoto_2024-11-20_19-23-01.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Df37f88db-ae29-4df6-a29e-74e7f16bf893&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=2254a8d&sv=2)

假如我们是要在Solana发币，就切换Solana链，然后进入Solana发币页面

### **3、Solana连接钱包**

进入之后，第一步就是连接钱包。我们点击右上角的连接钱包，之后选择phantom

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252Fn2pgEwAAiUZvLgtVhMtb%252F%25E8%25BF%259E%25E6%258E%25A5%25E9%2592%25B1%25E5%258C%2585.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dca64bf64-201f-4392-9d9c-d4cc0bebdc1f&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=2f7bd0a1&sv=2)

大家不要担心说，为什么没有OKX钱包选项？或者Phantom能不能连接到OKX？我可以负责任的告诉大家，完全可以兼容。只需要点击Phantom，就会自动匹配欧易，所以不用担心，直接选择就可以。

连接成功后，右上角会出现钱包的地址

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FR4Or9ipuvAcmJCHNQNDO%252F%25E8%25BF%259E%25E6%258E%25A5%25E6%2588%2590%25E5%258A%259F.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D51e64179-dc0a-45c8-a082-038f7a95390c&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=352d5146&sv=2)

这样网站打开默认是发币页面，如果您想进行其他操作，可以点击左上角的菜单栏，就能看到不同的工具选项

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FNmEFeIlPbRQrnSu470pF%252F%25E8%258F%259C%25E5%258D%2595%25E6%25A0%258F.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D3d90ca88-63ca-44cc-b703-71149c85c97d&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=75f549d8&sv=2)
