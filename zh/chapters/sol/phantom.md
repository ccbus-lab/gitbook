# 幻影钱包Phantom安装教程

Solana生态最常见的钱包

Phantom中文称之为“幻影钱包”或“幽灵钱包”，是一个浏览器插件，可以让我们在 Solana 区块链上存储、发送、接收、质押和交易代币。

目前，Phantom已成为用户参与Solana区块链时的首选钱包。如果你想使用PandaTool工具发行代币，就必须先安装Phantom钱包，下面给大家介绍一下安装教程：

  1. 首先打开 Phantom 钱包官网：https://phantom.app/，点击“Download For Chrome”。


![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FuPjocCuTFmcsXPi3nA3G%252F%25E9%2592%25B1%25E5%258C%2585%25E5%25AE%2598%25E7%25BD%2591.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D27eda4e4-dbc9-4a5c-8348-e581d26755a4&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=e95206fd&sv=2)

  1. 如下图所示，点击“添加至Chrome”


![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252F4dm0gTWAJFjSQ9ns6Xro%252F%25E8%25B0%25B7%25E6%25AD%258C%25E6%258F%2592%25E4%25BB%25B6.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3De06c6a53-daf8-4969-bbc6-5fb7a86f05e4&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=5263a6e3&sv=2)

  1. 然后会弹出提示“添加扩展程序”，点击添加


![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FjY9jT2lkW9vwnIXtWqOb%252F%25E6%25B7%25BB%25E5%258A%25A0%25E6%2589%25A9%25E5%25B1%2595%25E7%25A8%258B%25E5%25BA%258F.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dccf6791b-9879-4f9a-a6ad-0dea7c139fa5&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=cca41b72&sv=2)

  1. 添加之后，会让你进行选择：如果你还没有sol钱包，就可以立即创建一个新钱包。如果你在TP等其他平台已经创建了钱包，就可以通过私钥/助记词导入钱包，这里我们选择创建钱包：


![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FlKrFDKi2uDxohAdMqWSc%252F%25E5%2588%259B%25E5%25BB%25BA%25E6%2596%25B0%25E9%2592%25B1%25E5%258C%2585.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Db89975c3-33eb-449d-8c4d-0f90061b868f&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=36051085&sv=2)

  1. 之后需要创建一个钱包密码，这里和小狐狸一样


![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FsE9flCVtGnd6GpYJarxd%252F%25E9%2592%25B1%25E5%258C%2585%25E5%25AF%2586%25E7%25A0%2581.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D66f22735-1936-43f3-a9c7-ec59b07966e1&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=14939ded&sv=2)

  1. 保存你的钱包助记词，确保周围没有摄像头或者其他人


![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FCSDadQS6sLTUVD6rohHK%252F%25E4%25BF%259D%25E5%25AD%2598%25E5%258A%25A9%25E8%25AE%25B0%25E8%25AF%258D.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D22f1e653-0bb6-4aa7-98d3-3cff8becec84&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=44aaa323&sv=2)

  1. 至此，Phantom钱包就算注册安装完成了。你可以通过浏览器右上角的插件图标，找到你的钱包


![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FGn8QRw1qIIWsmQU98fkL%252F%25E6%2589%25BE%25E5%2588%25B0%25E9%2592%25B1%25E5%258C%2585.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D2457b119-67eb-4586-aed3-e4f8aed0da00&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=537260ac&sv=2)

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FbmEOqwRmahNNoLZ0hnPQ%252F%25E6%2589%25BE%25E5%2588%25B0%25E9%2592%25B1%25E5%258C%25852.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Ddc73c0b5-2580-47da-9a77-77d9debbfd3a&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=e3346b48&sv=2)

  1. 钱包创建完成后，我们复制SOL地址，再从交易所提SOL代币到这个地址，就能开始发币或者交易了


![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FSI0PK1oJ2NpTaJDhag6d%252Fsol%25E5%259C%25B0%25E5%259D%2580%25E5%25A4%258D%25E5%2588%25B6.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dd983b020-9044-4356-be01-7cb86fc65d7e&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=271683cf&sv=2)

注意事项

  1. 安装钱包必须在翻墙状态下，没有VPN的话，不建议大家操作

  2. Phantom钱包不仅支持Sol链，也支持以太坊、马蹄链，所以创建钱包之后会有3个地址。大家提币或者转账，一定要选择复制Solana地址，不要选错了
