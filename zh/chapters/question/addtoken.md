# 钱包怎么添加代币？

在各类钱包里添加代币的教程

很多人在发了币之后，不知道怎么在钱包里找到。其实很简单，用钱包添加一下就可以了。

本篇文章将教大家，如何在钱包里搜索并添加代币

### 1、TokenPocket钱包（TP钱包）

首先，我们需要打开对应的钱包地址和对应的链，然后点击**+** 按钮，输入**合约地址** ，就能看到了

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252Fxd47UkW0tPxAp58QikN0%252FTP%25E9%2592%25B1%25E5%258C%2585%25E6%25B7%25BB%25E5%258A%25A0%25E4%25BB%25A3%25E5%25B8%2581.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dc6d85a6a-aded-4293-af0c-c90835202be4&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=9c17da5e&sv=2)

如果输入合约地址找不到，还可以通过自定义代币添加。点击自定义代币，然后点击添加，输入合约地址，就会自动匹配了

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252Fp1ilVan3gmT3y1LXztsJ%252FTP%25E9%2592%25B1%25E5%258C%2585%25E8%2587%25AA%25E5%25AE%259A%25E4%25B9%2589%25E4%25BB%25A3%25E5%25B8%2581.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D808f6009-1628-4773-93e9-4174c72dbf44&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=b62baaed&sv=2)

### 2、欧易Web3钱包

OKX Web3钱包添加代币的流程与TP差不多，直接在钱包页面点击添加，搜索合约地址就可以了

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FdXNaj8k4PHZk92Ex7JOc%252FOKX%25E9%2592%25B1%25E5%258C%2585%25E6%25B7%25BB%25E5%258A%25A0%25E4%25BB%25A3%25E5%25B8%2581.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dd834eaac-699e-49af-af7b-5241a1190f29&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=f75690f7&sv=2)

### 4、小狐狸钱包MetaMask

小狐狸钱包与以上几个钱包不同，需要通过导入代币，来进行添加。

如果您使用的是小狐狸插件，可以点击导入后，输入合约地址来添加

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FWDqa2cEdkS8HrItC2esC%252F%25E5%25B0%258F%25E7%258B%2590%25E7%258B%25B8%25E6%25B7%25BB%25E5%258A%25A0%25E4%25BB%25A3%25E5%25B8%2581.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D6e14a9e6-064e-48ae-baaf-1b44281f4fd3&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=c377fe45&sv=2)
