# 手机TP钱包发币教程

使用TP钱包进行一键发币

呼入鉴于很多小伙伴没有电脑，或者不太会用电脑一键发币，所以这篇教程是告诉大家，如何使用手机上的TP钱包进行一键发币：

第一步：在手机上打开`TP钱包`，点击左上角`切换网络`

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FSdj67rlkbhp2IdUaVEAp%252F%25E6%2589%2593%25E5%25BC%2580TP%25E9%2592%25B1%25E5%258C%2585.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D4542486f-e510-4ca4-b444-e92117a8f393&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=34796976&sv=2)

TP钱包

第二步：选择你要创建合约的链以及钱包地址，这里以`币安链`为例

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FH3RphCnDh1aPxmDPO9kI%252F%25E5%2588%2587%25E6%258D%25A2%25E7%25BD%2591%25E7%25BB%259C.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D8d412c72-14fb-441c-a5cb-bcf4a5c952b4&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=ebca74c5&sv=2)

选择创建合约的钱包地址

第三步：点击下方第三个按钮`发现`，进入TP钱包内置浏览器

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FkdqzfmXsWi6b9xFRGl8q%252F%25E5%258F%2591%25E7%258E%25B0DAPP.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Da0313c95-4607-454b-bdac-f9c19c7439ae&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=b03d4d8c&sv=2)

发现DAPP

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FEVqUvSNAZ2zuTbG5JZzl%252F%25E8%25BF%259B%25E5%2585%25A5%25E5%2586%2585%25E7%25BD%25AE%25E6%25B5%258F%25E8%25A7%2588%25E5%2599%25A8.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D9f0c2a59-5218-467f-b01c-9d1c9840d428&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=fe30a8d9&sv=2)

进入钱包内置浏览器

第四步：输入`PandaTool`网址：pandatool.org

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FT36NtgazomKA1Tg2lBsK%252F%25E6%2590%259C%25E7%25B4%25A2pandatool.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Ddfb8ee16-e5c5-40c9-a9e9-a25e99f7e180&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=e0faf8ed&sv=2)

输入网站链接

第五步：进入PandaTool官网，选择左上角菜单，找到`发行代币`

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FLznCAr1eRKVwOjkxsz8I%252F%25E8%25BF%259B%25E5%2585%25A5%25E5%25AE%2598%25E7%25BD%2591.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D95687863-20aa-45ed-bb94-66a7e5ce5d0b&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=6530daa2&sv=2)

找到官网菜单

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252F181a7JRiqGmwNOYblYbB%252F%25E9%2580%2589%25E6%258B%25A9%25E5%2588%259B%25E5%25BB%25BA%25E4%25BB%25A3%25E5%25B8%2581.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D009df063-20e9-4570-b7ed-81dd26c96fee&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=628870f7&sv=2)

发行代币

第六步：选择你要发行的代币类型，这里以`标准代币`为例

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252Fjx8IPx7wic81Xu8jsFgK%252F%25E9%2580%2589%25E6%258B%25A9%25E6%25A0%2587%25E5%2587%2586%25E4%25BB%25A3%25E5%25B8%2581.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D3112b5ff-5638-4835-9988-88df99e5eebd&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=2de2ec3c&sv=2)

标准代币

第七步：按照提示填写`代币信息`

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FgGQRMy9i1tjgWPzfLcAy%252F%25E5%25A1%25AB%25E5%2586%2599%25E4%25BB%25A3%25E5%25B8%2581%25E4%25BF%25A1%25E6%2581%25AF.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Db8688167-fbe0-4ecb-b4f0-64b66bf19ad5&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=de778fb1&sv=2)

第八步：点击`创建合约`，进行二次确认

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252F4gyxZC65MQPx4rtGPMtV%252F%25E8%25BF%259B%25E8%25A1%258C%25E5%2588%259B%25E5%25BB%25BA%25E5%2590%2588%25E7%25BA%25A6.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dec5e9fa7-5bdd-4f83-beec-48beb29c397f&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=d3711047&sv=2)

创建合约

![](https://help.pandatool.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1885923539-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FnmLBiMxr5iATgeZGW8in%252Fuploads%252FTrwQOc7I1PITvAb8rDue%252F%25E5%2588%259B%25E5%25BB%25BA%25E5%2590%2588%25E7%25BA%25A6.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D7e9f9e54-50dd-474d-831f-a5f9e5d151e1&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=b62e28c2&sv=2)
